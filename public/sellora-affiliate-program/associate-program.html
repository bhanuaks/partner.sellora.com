Sellora Associate Program

The Sellora Associate Program is a dynamic influencer marketing initiative designed to empower individuals, businesses, blogger and influencers to promote products available on the Sellora marketplace. This Associate program provides a unique opportunity for Associates to earn commissions by referring new customers, driving sales, and enhancing Sellora’s reach across different product categories.

The Sellora Associate Program is built to help Associates earn income by sharing Sellora products with their audience. Associates will have access to marketing tools, product links, and promotional resources to facilitate their success in generating sales. The program is simple, flexible, and designed to benefit everyone from individual, bloggers to large-scale marketing agencies.

Key Features

Diverse Product Catalog

Promote products across categories like electronics, fashion, beauty, home decor, and more.

Track sales, commissions, and performance in real time with a user-friendly interface.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the Sellora Associate Program?

The Sellora Associate Program is an influencer marketing initiative designed to help individuals, businesses, and influencers earn commissions by promoting Selloras products. Associates can share unique referral links, and every successful sale made through their link earns them a commission

2. How does the program work?

Once you join, you ll receive a personalized Associates dashboard with unique referral links for products available on the Sellora marketplace. You can share these links through your preferred channels (social media, blogs, websites, etc.). For every sale generated through your referral link, you ll earn a commission

3. Who can join the Sellora Associate Program?

Anyone with a platform or audience can join, including

  • Social media influencer
  • Bloggers and content creator
  • Digital marketer
  • Entrepreneurs and businesses

4. Is there any cost to join the program?

No, joining the Sellora Associate Program is completely free.

5. What kind of support will I receive as an Associates?

As part of the program, Associates will receive

  • Access to marketing resources such as banners, product images, and descriptions
  • Personalized support from Selloras Associates team
  • Training materials to help you succeed in promoting products effectively.

6. How do I track my referrals and earnings?

You ll get access to an Associate dashboard where you can

  • Monitor clicks and sales generated through your referral links.
  • Track your earnings in real time.
  • Access detailed performance reports.

7. What is the commission structure?

The commission structure is competitive and varies by product category. Full details about commissions will be provided when you join the program.

8. How and when do I get paid?

Payments are made monthly, provided you meet the minimum payout threshold. Payment options include bank transfer or other methods communicated by the Sellora team.

9. Can I promote any product on the Sellora marketplace?

Yes, you can promote any eligible product listed on the Sellora marketplace. The dashboard will allow you to generate referral links for individual products.

10. What marketing channels can I use to promote products?

You can share referral links through a variety of platforms, including:

  • Social media (Instagram, What s App Channel, Facebook, X, LinkedIn, etc.
  • Personal blogs or websiteõ
  • Email campaigns
  • YouTube videos or other content sharing platforms

11. Are there any restrictions for Associates?

Yes, Associates must follow Selloras Associates program guidelines. Prohibited practices include spamming, false advertising, and using unethical methods to generate sales.

12. How do I sign up?

To get started, visit the Sellora Associate Program page and fill out the application form. After approval, you ll gain access to the Associates dashboard and all the necessary tools to begin promoting.

13. Can I contact support for help?

Of course! A dedicated support team is available to assist with questions or concerns. You can contact them directly through your Associate dashboard.
